How to Play?

What you are betting on is the result of a 100-faced dice. The result of the dice is limited to 1-100. The result of the dice needs to be lower than the number you choose. If the result of the dice is lower than your number, you win! Example: The number that you choose is 61. When the result of the dice is less than 61 (1-60), you win. If the result is greater than or equal to 61 (61-100), you lost

1. Prepare your Seele account. Please follow tutorial to create an account if you don't have

2. Press the login button to login with public and private key

3. Select your bet amount, this is the amount of Seele you want to bet

4. Slide the slider to change your chance of winning by guessing the upper limit of the dice number

5. Click "Roll" to bet. Click “Accept” to confirm, and the bet is successful

6. If the dice number rolled is lower than dice number you guess, you win and gets the payout

7. If you receive a notification that the transaction has failed, please check if you have enough Seele to make the bet

You can check your Seele balance next to the Roll button. The table below the slider bar shows the recent game status and your bet details for all players worldwide.

** As the game is run on the Seele Smart Contract, it is proved to be fair and cannot be cheated. By our unique Encryption technology, the result of rolling will not be changed by any means.
